This is a YouTube page

A YouTube video is a digital file uploaded to the YouTube platform, where it can be viewed, shared, and interacted with by a global audience. Each video on YouTube typically includes a title, description, and tags that help users find and understand the content. Videos can cover a vast range of topics, from educational tutorials and entertainment to personal vlogs and professional presentations. Viewers can engage with the content through comments, likes, and shares, and subscribe to channels for regular updates. YouTube videos are often accompanied by a thumbnail image, which serves as a visual preview of the content. The platform also provides analytics for creators to track viewership statistics and engagement, helping them refine their content and reach their target audience more effectively.
